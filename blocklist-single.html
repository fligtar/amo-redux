Skype extension has been blocked for your protection.

Why was it blocked?
High volume of crashes and severe performance issues. For more information, please read our announcement.
Who is affected?
Users of any version of the Skype extension in all versions of Firefox.
What does this mean?
When Mozilla becomes aware of add-ons, plugins, or other third-party software that seriously compromises Firefox security, stability, or performance and meets certain criteria, the software may be blocked from general use. [softblock]This means users are strongly encouraged to disable the problematic add-on or plugin, but may choose to continue using it if they accept the risks described.[/softblock][hardblock]This means the problematic add-on or plugin will be automatically disabled and no longer usable.[/hardblock] For more information, please read this support article.
